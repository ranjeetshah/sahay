
# 🌍 SAHAY – Help Without Money

## 💡 Idea
A platform where people help each other by:
- Giving advice
- Teaching skills
- Listening to problems

No money involved.

---

## 🎯 Goal
Build a community where:
- Help = value
- Trust = currency

---

## ⚠️ Problem Today
- People feel alone
- Advice platforms are chaotic
- No trust system

---

## 🚀 Solution
- Structured help system
- Reputation-based trust
- Community moderation

---

## 🔮 Future Vision
- Fully open-source
- Federated (multiple servers)
- No central authority
